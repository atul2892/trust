About

Pragati Parivar

Welcome to Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd., your number one source for all Business Training & Networking needs. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best of business training and networking opportunities, with a focus on Business Trainings. Pragati Parivar is brand name of Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd for providing business training and networking services.

Founded in February 2016 by Mr.Praveen Narang with 2 more partners,Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd. has come a long way from its beginnings in East Delhi.
