Pragati Parivar - Business Training and Networking Platform

Welcome to Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd., your number one source for all Business Training & Networking needs. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best of business training and networking opportunities, with a focus on Business Trainings. Pragati Parivar is brand name of Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd for providing business training and networking services.

Founded in February 2016 by Mr.Praveen Narang with 2 more partners,Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd. has come a long way from its beginnings in East Delhi. When Mr.Praveen Narang first started out,his passion for providing platform to businessman and professionals to learn and grow with each other and drove them to reach heights in business and profession so that Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd.

Can offer you “the world’s most advanced training and networking platform.We now serve customers all over Delhi and NCR, and are thrilled that we’re able to turn our passion into reality.Pragati Training Services Pvt Ltd is being run by professional team comprising of individuals with vast experience in profession and business.We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them toyou. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

PRAGATI PARIVAR FOR BUSNIESS

The best business is referred business – That’s why our Pragati Parivar really do work for our members. You can get new customers easier and quicker than your competition. Securing your exclusive place at your chosen Pragati Parivar means you block out the competition, giving you the best chance of gaining referrals. Our members are consistently referring business to each other, so you could have up to 80 members promoting and recommending your products and services by word-of-mouth. As well as improving presentation skills; boosting your confidence and gaining referrals, you will from strong relationship with professional people who are there to help you.

Pragati Parivar members get to know, like and trust each other and are happy to recommend your products and services, which generates quality referrals, for you to turn into good quality business. Our monthly meetings provide the platform to network in a professionally structured environment that is relaxed and pressure free.

The first Pragati Parivar Wing opened in February 2016. We now have 4 Wings of Pragat Parivar. Pragati Parivar meetings are held every month, giving you plenty of time to generate referrals.

Every member is given 40 seconds to do a quick presentation about their business. Every month members are given 10 minutes to carry out a more comprehensive ‘Showcase’ presentation about their business in a year. There is NO PRESSURE on members or visitors to give referrals at every single meeting, as this only encourages people to give worthless referrals that are not worth pursuing.

Our members are more interested in Quality NOT Quantity. Don’t take our word for it, why not attend as a visitor and experience Pragati for yourself?
